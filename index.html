SUSPENDED IN DEEP SPACE · NO RULES · NO ESCAPE
You have been dropped into a neon arena floating above the void.
5 AI challengers have been activated.
The platform has no walls. The edge is the only rule.
Survive. Dominate. Be the last one standing.

Stand on the KING ZONE (center) to earn +2 pts/sec
Collect POWER-UP ORBS scattered across the arena

Eliminate all 5 challengers OR outlast them all
Fall off the edge = respawn at center, -25 pts
